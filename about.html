About us

Get to know everything about us in a concise manner

Who we are ?

Welcome to KrazyKnots! We are a team of passionate macrame enthusiasts who love to create and showcase beautiful handmade products and we also feature products from other talented macrame artisans Our website features a range of macrame products, from plant hangers and wall hangings to keychains and jewelry.

What is our Vision?

Our vision is to create a marketplace for macrame lovers who loves to buy macrame products and also for all small macrame product sellers to make it big, as we also feature products from other talented macrame artisans apart from our own manufactured items. We believe that by bringing together a diverse range of macrame products and makers, we can help support the growth of the macrame community by helping small macrame product sellers reach a wider audience and offer our customers a wider selection of unique, high-quality products.

what do we promise?

We take great pride in the craftsmanship and quality of our products. Each item is carefully handcrafted using only the finest materials, and we are committed to providing our customers with exceptional customer service. Overall, we are valuable to both buyers and sellers because we offer a convenient, reliable, and trustworthy platform to connect and transact. Whether you are a customer looking for unique macrame products or a small macrame product seller looking to expand your reach, we are here to help.

Thank you for visiting our website, and we hope you find the perfect macrame product to add to your home or gift to a loved one. Happy Shopping!
